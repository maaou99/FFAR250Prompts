A manifesto of a Creative Coder

Purpose


Your code is the building block of your defined accomplishment. Set your goal and then code. Whether for a social, cultural or political issue, spread a message with your art made by code. Therefore, your final result serves a specific purpose generated through code.

Accessibility


Code is available to all genders, ages and professional situations. Your journey in learning to code was through code found online, made by people who were once in the same situation as you. Share your program with other curious seekers to let them gain knowledge in code.

Clarity

Since your code serves others, anyone must understand it, regardless of their level of expertise. To do so, the overall structure of your program must be cohesive and logical. Whether it is a website or game, the user must also navigate smoothly through what the code displays.

Interactivity



To become a creative thinker and coder, one must ensure that their piece interacts physically with the real world. The audience must feel involved and understand the artists intention.
