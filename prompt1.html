Language is everywhere. we allow it to share our thoughts, express our knowledge and enhance or destroy relationships. Language is at all times present, and will persistently be. However, so are computers. How do computers display desired information? Well, we tell it do so. We speak to the computer a certain language and it “magically” responds. It’s a language the majority are not aware of, but are unknowingly in touch with it on the daily. A language we are not necessarily asked to learn, but are pushed to trust its semantics. A language a human can write and a computer can interpret is therefore considered an essential material for our modern era. What is this language called? Code. However, before we bring up the involvement of code for an artist, we must emphasize on the similarities between language and code.
