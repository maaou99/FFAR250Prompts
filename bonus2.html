Thoughts of an overthinker




On my way to the bus stop

"Its 7.45am. “Have a forgotten anything? My computer? My charger? My Wallet? Cool. Oh there is ice. Will I fall? Little steps mariam. Little steps. Wow now I’m walking like a penguin. Let me double check If I have lunch. My bag is a black hole. Finally I’ve arrived to the bus stop.”



In the bus

"Look straight and DO NOT make eye contact. Oops, I just made eye contact. Look at them until they stop looking at you. Wait they are still looking. Okay now look down. How many bus stops left? I feel like a sardine. Finally I stepped out of the bus. Now I can smell fresh air."



In the metro

"Go on your phone. Do not look at your phone. I’ll just look out the subway window. I feel sleepy. Let me just close my eyes for a few seconds. Nevermind I’ll just look at the floor. Ok so let me do quick recap of the day. I have to continue building my website, do this, do that, start reading the article, then finish this and then finish that. You can do it and you are capable. Hold on, I just crossed path with a stranger I saw in my bus. It’s destiny. Are we already in guy concordia metro station?"
